Merriam Webster defines Role Playing Game as:

an adventure game in which players advance a character through a series of battles or quests to gain experience, strength, and skill as part of a progressive narrative arc usually depicting the hero's journey.

Role Playing Games started with Dungeons & Dragons in 1974. D&D would be considered a TTRPG (table tob role playing game). The popularity and format of D&D set the foundation for Role Playing Games as a genre.

Featuring:

  • D-20 decision making (you need to roll a 20 to continue)
  • Story progression over the course of a whole campaign
  • Gradual improvement of players as they progress and gain experience
  • Immersion through the story telling of the GM, and involvement of the players
